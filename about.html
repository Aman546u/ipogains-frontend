About IPOGains

Empowering investors with real-time IPO data and analysis.

Our Mission

At IPOGains, our mission is to simplify the IPO investing process for everyone. We provide transparent, accurate, and timely information about upcoming, ongoing, and listed IPOs in the Indian stock market.

We believe that access to high-quality financial data should be available to every retail investor, helping them make informed decisions and achieve their financial goals.

What We Offer

  • Live GMP Tracking: Get the latest Grey Market Premium updates for all active IPOs.
  • Subscription Status: Real-time subscription data across Retail, QIB, and NII categories.
  • Allotment Status: Fast and direct links to check your IPO allotment status.
  • SME & Mainboard: Comprehensive coverage of both Mainboard and SME IPOs.
