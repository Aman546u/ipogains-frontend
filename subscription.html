Understanding Subscription Categories

QIB (Qualified Institutions)

Includes FII, DFI, and Mutual Funds investing in massive volumes.

High net-worth individuals (₹2L+). Usually the most aggressive bidders.

Retail Investors

Common investors (RII) applying for amounts up to ₹2 Lakh.

Shareholder

Eligible existing shareholders of the promoter company as of the record date.
